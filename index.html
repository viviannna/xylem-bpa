Help Us Make an Impact


Getting started can often seem overwhelming. That's why we compiled a list of a few eay ways you can help make a change. Simply click on the tile that piques your interest and read more!


In Your Home


Garden

Compost has great benefits for you and the Earth! It can help your garden bloom while reducing the emission of greenhouse gasses. Win-win.

Waste Basket

Revisiting the three Rs can help reduce the amount of items placed in a landfills annually. Many of the items in your own home are shockingly easy to recylce.

Kitchen

Food produces 17% of all total carbon emissions in the home. Making small changes to your diet and your kitchen can greatly assist in reducing your personal carbon footprint.


In Your Community


Commute

Simply changing the way you get to work or school can help you to engage in your community.

Groceries

Making small changes to your diet and your kitchen can greatly assist in reducing your personal carbon footprint.

Service

Volunteering with your local county tree planting initiative or your local arborist increases the greenery in your area.


In Your City


Solar Energy

Encouraging your city to enact incentives for homes and businesses to add solar panels can help your area shine.

Plastic Reduction

Promoting reusable items such as bags and refillable waters can keep your home litter and plastic free.

Local Policy

Getting involved and petitioning your local government can make a sizable change.
