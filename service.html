Serve Your Community
and Your Planet


Getting involved in community service in your local city, county, or community has tremendous benefits for your mental health and the environement.

Roll Up Your Sleeves


Sometimes all you need to help the planet is your time. Rolling up your sleeves and getting involved in the needs and improvement of your community is a great way to build bonds, ensure future sucess, and help enact change. Click on the tiles below to read some of our suggestions on where to volunteer.



Tree Planting


Grab a shovel and plant new trees and shrubbery in local areas such as parks and schools.

Water Clean-Up


Help pick up litter and plastic waste that clutters waterway systems harming animal life and drinking quality.

Don't Mess With Texas Trash Off


Volunteer to clear litter off of local roadsides.
